KWYO Audition INFO

  • Prospective KWYO members of all 4 orchestras are admitted via auditions
  • KWYO in-person (live) auditions are an opportunity to meet our faculty and to learn what we offer
  • Returning members are expected to audition each season. The only exception to this policy are members of the Preludium Strings returning to our entry level orchestra for 2nd year of instruction
  • Majority of KWYO auditions are scheduled for the end of May / early June every year. Exact dates are posted after March break. Please keep a time-block open on the day of your choice. Exact times are assigned by the KWYO staff.
  • A limited opportunity to join the orchestras might be scheduled for early September subject to seat availability in an orchestra
  • Subject to circumstances a video-audition opportunity is available. Please contact KWYO for more info on video-auditions
  • Your in-person audition will take between 8-10 minutes
  • Conductors and professional musician from the community will evaluate the following areas of your performance: tone quality, secure intonation, accurate pitches, rhythm & tempo, dynamic range and the style of music played
  • KWYO is actively seeking young musicians playing viola, double bass, oboe, bassoon, French horn, trumpet, trombone and tuba. Auditions on those instruments are available throughout the season
  • To register for an audition a candidate is required to submit their Audition Application
  • KWYO Audition Application available from this site opens in mid April of each season
  • Please refer to the audition requirements to determine which KWYO orchestra best matches your instrumental skill level
  • Required audition materials consist of scales, a solo of own choice and selected orchestral excerpts (except for Preludium Strings)
  • Audition results are communicated to candidates within 72 h following their audition day
  • Successful candidates are expected to complete their KWYO enrollment to secure their placement in an orchestra upon receiving the acceptance notification
