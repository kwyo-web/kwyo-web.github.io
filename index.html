Welcome to KWYO

KWYO is a community-based, non-profit organization focused on enhancing music education in the Waterloo region. We offer youth continuous, high-quality orchestral instruction and rewarding performance opportunities in a fun, challenging, and safe environment.

The Kitchener Waterloo Youth Orchestra acknowledges that we operate on the traditional territory of the Attawandaron (Neutral), Anishinaabeg, and Haudenosaunee peoples. We honor their enduring stewardship of the land and waters, and we are grateful for the knowledge they continue to share. As an organization dedicated to youth, music, and community, we are committed to ongoing learning and supporting reconciliation in meaningful ways.
