Welcome to Kitchener Waterloo Youth Orchestra!

KWYO is a community based non-for-profit organization committed to supplementing music education by bringing to Waterloo region's youth, ages 5 to 22, a continuity of a high standard orchestral instruction and rewarding performance opportunities in a fun, challenging and safe environment.

Our mission is to bring together in a creative process of ensemble performance the music-loving students to empower them to grow and to develop numerous transferable skills needed to thrive as an individual and a team member. KWYO's leading ideas of inclusivity, equality, versatility and accessibility provides paths for nurturing thriving and compassionate community members, arts enthusiasts, as well as professional and community musicians.

KWYO activities for four orchestras in progressive levels run from September through April, in two Waterloo locations. Program details including rehearsals and concerts schedules and cost TBC soon. Students are accepted via audition. The live auditions to join an orchestra for the 2024-25 season will be held in the 2nd and 3rd week of June. For requirements, info and to register for an audition and any other questions contact us at kw.community.yo@gmail.com

Our ORCHESTRAS

Preludium Strings - entry level orchestra

  • opening skill level RCM 1 / Suzuki 1
  • Instruments: violin, viola, cello and double bass
  • suggested minimum age 6 years old
  • Rossana Paz, conductor

Youth Strings - junior level orchestra

  • opening skill level RCM 3 or its equivalent in Suzuki
  • Instruments: violin, viola, cello and double bass
  • Suggested minimum age 8 years old
  • Matthew Jones, conductor

Youth Sinfonia - intermediate level orchestra

  • opening skill level RCM 6 or its equivalent in Suzuki
  • Instruments: violin, viola, cello and double bass
  • Suggested minimum age 10 years old
  • Ian Whitman, conductor

Youth Orchestra - advanced level symphony

  • opening skill level RCM 8 or its equivalent in Suzuki
  • Instruments: violin, viola, cello and double bass, flute, oboe, clarinet, bassoon, French horn, trumpet, trombone & bass trombone, tuba, timpani and other percussion, harp
  • Suggested minimum age 12 years old
  • Matthew Jones, conductor
