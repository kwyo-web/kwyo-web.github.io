KWYO Audition REQUIREMENTS


Audition requirements details are listed under each orchestra and generally include:

  • Scales
  • Solo piece(s)
  • Short orchestral excerpts for most orchestras / instruments
To prepare orchestral excerpts, we recommend listening to recordings and consulting your private instructor or ensemble leader.

PRELUDIUM STRINGS

Entry level string orchestra. Suggested minimum age: 6 years old. To join our entry-level string orchestra, a candidate should have been taking private music lessons for about a year and have a basic understanding of music notation. Suggested opening skill level RCM* 1 or its equivalent in Suzuki.

Instruments

  • Violin
  • Viola
  • Cello
  • Double Bass

Preludium Strings Audition Requirements

  • G major or C major scale - 1 or 2 octaves
  • A solo piece of the candidate's choice that best demonstrates their current skill level, performed without piano accompaniment

* Royal Conservatory of Music

YOUTH STRINGS

Junior level strings orchestra. Suggested minimum age: 8 years old. Instruments: violin, viola, cello and double bass. Opening skill level RCM* 3 or its equivalent in Suzuki.

Youth Strings Audition Requirements

Violin

  • Scales: D major, 2 octaves & C minor, 2 octaves
  • A solo piece of the candidate's choice (1-2 min long) that best demonstrates their current skill level, performed without piano accompaniment.
  • Selected Orchestral Excerpts
  • Orchestral Excerpts: 2025-26 Youth Strings Auditions VIOLIN

Viola

  • Scales: C major, 2 octaves & D minor, 1 or 2 octaves
  • A solo piece of the candidate's choice (1-2 min long) that best demonstrates their current skill level, performed without piano accompaniment.
  • Orchestral Excerpts: 2025-26 Youth Strings Auditions VIOLA

Cello

  • Scales: G major, 2 octaves & D minor, 2 octaves
  • A solo piece of the candidate's choice (1-2 min long) that best demonstrates their current skill level, performed without piano accompaniment.
  • Orchestral Excerpts: 2025-26 Youth Strings Auditions CELLO

Double Bass

  • 2 scales of own choice, 1 octave
  • A solo piece of the candidate's choice (1-2 min long) that best demonstrates their current skill level, performed without piano accompaniment.
  • Orchestral Excerpts: 2025-26 Youth Strings Auditions DOUBLE BASS

* Royal Conservatory of Music

YOUTH SINFONIA

Intermediate level string & wind (NEW) orchestra. Suggested minimum age: 10 years old.

Instruments:

  • Strings (violin, viola, cello and double bass)
    • Opening skill level of RCM Level 5 or its equivalent in Suzuki.
  • Woodwinds (flute, oboe, clarinet & bassoon)
    • Suggested opening skill level of RCM 6 / middle school or secondary school grade 9-10 band experience.
  • Brass (French horn, trumpet, trombone and tuba)
    • Suggested opening skill level of RCM 4 / middle school or secondary school grade 9-10 band experience.

  • Orchestral Excerpts: 2025-26 Youth Sinfonia Auditions VIOLIN

Viola

  • Scales: C major, 2 octaves & D minor, 2 octaves
  • A solo piece of the candidate's choice (1-2 minutes long, or a section of a longer piece) that best demonstrates their current skill level, performed without piano accompaniment.
  • Orchestral Excerpts: 2025-26 Youth Sinfonia Auditions VIOLA

Cello

  • Scales: D major, 2 octaves & G minor, 2 octaves

Double Bass

Woodwinds

YOUTH ORCHESTRA

Advanced level symphony orchestra. Suggested minimum age: 12 years old.

Instruments:

  • Strings (violin, viola, cello, double bass & harp)
    • Opening skill level of RCM Level 8 or its equivalent in Suzuki.
  • Woodwinds (flute / piccolo, oboe, clarinet & bassoon)
    • Suggested opening skill level of RCM 8 / senior band experience, ideally supplemented by private music lessons.
  • Brass (French horn, trumpet, trombone and tuba)
    • Suggested opening skill level of RCM 6 / secondary school grade 11-12 or university band experience, ideally supplemented by private music lessons.
  • Percussion (timpani, snare drum & xylophone)
    • Suggested opening skill level of RCM 6 / secondary school grade 11-12 or university band experience, ideally supplemented by private music lessons.

Youth Orchestra Audition Requirements

Strings

Violin

  • Scales: one major & one minor, 3 octaves, in 16th notes, 8 per bow, M.M. = 60 and corresponding arpeggios
  • A solo piece of the candidate's choice, 2-3 minutes long, or a part of a larger work that best demonstrates their current skill level, performed without piano accompaniment.
  • Orchestral Excerpts: 2025-26 YO Auditions VIOLIN

Violin - Concertmaster & Leadership Role Audition

  • Orchestral Excerpts: 2025-26 YO Auditions Concertmaster & Leadership Role
    • Candidates must audition in the spring to participate
    • Indicate your intention in your Audition Application.
    • Prepare the additional material required to perform in your violin audition which will be extended to approximately 15 minutes.
    • The second stage of the Concertmaster & Leadership Role auditions is scheduled for early September. Successful candidates will receive additional Stage 2 material before the summer, to be presented in the September audition.

Percussion

Candidates are required to supply their own mallets and drumsticks

Timpani

  • Demonstrate Single Stroke Roll to create long sustained sound in a Slow-to-Fast execution
  • A solo piece or a study of candidate's choice (1-2 minutes long) that best demonstrates their current skill level
  • Orchestral Excerpts (RCM 8 - optional): 2025-26 YO Auditions PERCUSSION

Snare Drum

  • Demonstrate the two rudiments in a Slow-Fast-Slow execution:
    • Double Stroke Roll
    • Single Paradiddle
  • A solo piece or a study of candidate's choice (1-2 minutes long) that best demonstrates their current skill level

Xylophone (optional)

  • 2 scales: one major & one minor of the candidate’s choice, 2 octaves
